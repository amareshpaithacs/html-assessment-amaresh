Frequently Asked Questions

How do I navigate this website?

Use the navigation bar at the top of every page. You can click on the links to go to different pages. On the main navigation page, you can also use the Table of Contents to jump to different sections.

Where can I download files?

Download links are available on the main navigation page. Click the download buttons to save the PDF guide or TXT notes to your computer.

How can I contact Amaresh?

You can send an email to amaresh@paithacs.com or call on +91 7799582309.

What technologies are used here?

This website is made using only pure HTML. No CSS, no JavaScript, no frameworks. It is done as part of an HTML Fundamentals Assessment.

How do I use the image map?

On the main navigation page, there is an image with clickable areas. Just click on any part of the image to be taken to that section of the page.
